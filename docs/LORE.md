# LORE.md — the REDTHREAD canon

This is the in-universe bible. It keeps copy, naming, and tone consistent across
the whole product. When you write user-facing text, write it from inside this
world.

## 1. The premise

REDTHREAD is an open dossier — a place where the people who notice things compare
notes. The conceit: every event is a pin, every pin can be connected to another,
and the truth is in the **pattern** the strings make. Members aren't "users,"
they're **operatives** working the same wall.

The site commits to the bit. But it also knows what it is — see Tone (§4).

## 3. Clearance rank ladder

Flavor + credibility, **not** a hard access gate in v1. Earned through
contribution and corroboration.

1. **Sheeple** — fresh initiate, still asleep.
2. **Awakened** — started connecting dots.
3. **Researcher** — contributes evidence regularly.
4. **Watchman** — trusted, high credibility.
5. **Handler** — community steward.
6. **Architect** — sees the whole board.

## 4. Tone guide — "straight-faced immersion with a knowing wink"

The voice commits to the world *and* occasionally lets the mask slip with dry
humor. Think: a narrator who absolutely believes, plus a footnote that knows
you're in on it. The wink is in the **copy**, never in hiding what a button does.

**Do**
- Deliver the absurd with a straight face: *"Your clearance level: [REDACTED]."*
- Reframe ordinary actions in-world: a 404 is *"FILE NOT FOUND // RECORD EXPUNGED."*
- Land an occasional dry wink: *"All files are collaborative speculation. Take
  nobody's word for it — not even ours."*
- Keep it playful and inclusive. We're having fun with the genre.

**Don't**
- Assert real conspiracy claims as fact, or target real living people. (See
  [`SECURITY_AND_MODERATION.md`](SECURITY_AND_MODERATION.md).)
- Let the theme cost clarity: error/empty states must still tell the user what
  happened and what to do.
- Be mean, paranoid-for-real, hateful, or alarmist. The menace is theatrical.
- Punch down or court extremist content. The wink keeps it light on purpose.

**Microcopy bank** (reuse for consistency)
- Auth gate: *"INITIATE to pull this thread."*
- Loading: *"DECRYPTING…"*
- Empty board: *"No evidence pinned yet. Someone has to go first."*
- Wire down: *"THE WIRE IS QUIET — source unreachable, retrying."*
- Footer: *"We shadow our names, for they are watching."*

## 5. Naming & voice

- The product is **REDTHREAD** (all caps in the wordmark; "RED" in red).
- Tagline: **"Pull the thread."**
- The maker is credited as **Kairos**. Operatives choose their own shadow names.
- Prefer terse, typed-memo cadence. Uppercase labels. Em dashes and ellipses for
  that withheld-information feeling. Numbers as *N° 0001*, years as *MMXXVI* where
  it adds flavor (but never where it harms clarity).
